Flet 0.86.0 fills in two pieces that declarative apps were really missing: routing and dialogs. Combined with smoother video, real-time audio, and a healthy round of bug fixes, this release moves the `@ft.component` programming model from "promising" to "production-ready for real apps".

Try it in your apps and share feedback in [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/flet-dev/flet/discussions) or on [Discord](https://discord.gg/dzWXP8SHG8).

Happy Flet-ing!
